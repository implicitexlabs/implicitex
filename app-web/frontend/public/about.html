ImplicitEx

About

ImplicitEx is a focused peer-to-peer USDC transfer platform. The intended production flow is non-custodial: users connect their own wallet, review recipient and amount details, approve USDC when required, and submit the transfer through the ImplicitEx smart contract.

The current public shell is demo-only while testnet validation is in progress. Live transfers remain disabled until the deployment, chain configuration, approval flow, and transaction lifecycle gates are complete.

The planned launch fee is a transparent 1% flat platform fee routed by the contract during a completed transfer.
