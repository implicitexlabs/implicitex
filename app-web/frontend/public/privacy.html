Privacy

The current ImplicitEx public shell is demo-only and does not execute live transfers. Wallet connection, when used, is initiated through the user's browser wallet provider.

ImplicitEx does not require custody of private keys. Users should never share seed phrases, private keys, or wallet recovery material with ImplicitEx or anyone claiming to provide support.

Public blockchain transactions are visible on their respective networks. Wallet addresses, transaction hashes, token transfers, fees, and contract interactions may be publicly observable after live execution is enabled.
