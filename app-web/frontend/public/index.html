How it works
01 — Connect

Your wallet, your keys. No account required.
ImplicitEx connects through your browser wallet. The platform does not ask for an email account, password, seed phrase, or private key.
02 — Send

Enter a recipient address and amount. The 1% platform fee is added on top — the recipient receives the full amount you enter.
The transfer preview shows the recipient, amount, platform fee, and total debit before any wallet action. You pay the amount plus the fee. The recipient receives the full amount.
03 — Confirm

Two wallet confirmations: one to authorize, one to send. Authorization alone does not move funds.
The first wallet prompt authorizes the ImplicitEx contract to transfer the exact USDC amount. The second sends it. You receive a receipt with a link to verify the transaction on Polygon.

      Fee & Routing

      The recipient amount and total wallet debit are different. The total debit is recipient amount plus platform fee. Approval, when needed, must cover the full total debit. Approval alone does not send funds.

      Preview Discipline

      Preview data is refreshed from the contract before any wallet prompt. Recipient, network, pause state, contract address, and USDC token checks remain separate execution gates.

      Network Note

      This transfer uses USDC on Polygon. Do not use this form to send Bitcoin, Ethereum mainnet USDC, bank USD, or unsupported assets. A valid-looking address does not prove the recipient controls it.

      Direct Send Warning

      Do not send USDC directly to the ImplicitEx contract address. Direct transfers may be unrecoverable. See Legal for current notices.
