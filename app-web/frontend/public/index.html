How it works
01 — Connect

Your wallet, your keys. No account required.
ImplicitEx connects through your browser wallet. The platform does not ask for an email account, password, seed phrase, or private key.
02 — Send

Enter a recipient address and USDC amount. Review the 1% fee before confirming.
The transfer preview shows the recipient, USDC amount, platform fee, and estimated gas context before any wallet confirmation is requested.
03 — Confirm

Approve and transfer in two on-chain steps. Receipt link provided on completion.
Token approval and transfer execution are separate wallet actions. When live transfers are enabled, completed transactions will include a network explorer receipt link.

Fee & Routing

ImplicitEx applies a 1% platform fee to completed transfers. The fee is routed by the smart contract to the configured treasury address at execution time — not held or reviewed manually by ImplicitEx. In preview mode, no transaction is submitted and no funds move.
