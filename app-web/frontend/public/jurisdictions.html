Jurisdiction Availability

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This page describes where ImplicitEx is intended to be available during MVP planning and where access is restricted. The current public site is a demo shell and testnet-preparation surface. Live transfers remain disabled until the applicable readiness gates are complete.

Intended availability and restricted jurisdictions may change at any time based on law, sanctions, infrastructure availability, service-provider support, blockchain-network support, risk review, or business judgment. Users are responsible for complying with the laws that apply to them.

Restricted by Platform Policy

ImplicitEx does not intend to support use by any person or entity that is located in, organized in, ordinarily resident in, controlled from, or acting on behalf of any restricted jurisdiction, sanctioned region, blocked person, or prohibited party.

Restricted Countries

  • Belarus
  • Cuba
  • Iran
  • North Korea
  • Russia
  • Syria

Restricted Regions

  • Crimea region of Ukraine
  • Donetsk region of Ukraine
  • Kherson region of Ukraine
  • Luhansk region of Ukraine
  • Zaporizhzhia region of Ukraine
  • Any other comprehensively sanctioned or embargoed region

Restricted Persons

  • Any person on an applicable sanctions list
  • Any person owned or controlled by a sanctioned person
  • Any person using the platform for a sanctioned person
  • Any person using the platform to evade sanctions or controls

The restricted list is intentionally conservative. It includes territory-wide sanctions and additional jurisdictions that ImplicitEx has chosen not to support for MVP risk-control reasons.
